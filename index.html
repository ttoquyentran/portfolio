Hello, I'm Quyen Tran

I have been a lecturer at Can Tho University since 2011. I received my PhD degree in computer science from Univ Rennes 1, ENSSAT, CNRS, IRISA in 2020. My research interests include big data algorithms and architectures, distributed and parallel databases, computer networks, web development and cybersecurity.
